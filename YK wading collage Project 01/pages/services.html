Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What types of events can I manage with EventFlow Pro?

EventFlow Pro is designed to handle a wide range of events, including conferences, workshops, seminars, corporate events, fundraisers, social gatherings, and more. Our flexible platform can be customized to meet the specific needs of virtually any event type.

Can I try EventFlow Pro before purchasing a plan?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial that gives you access to all features of our Professional plan. No credit card is required to start your trial, and you can upgrade to a paid plan at any time.

How secure is the payment processing system?

We take security seriously. Our payment processing system is PCI DSS compliant and uses industry-standard encryption to protect sensitive information. We partner with trusted payment gateways to ensure secure transactions for you and your attendees.

Can I customize the look and feel of my event pages?

Absolutely! All plans include customization options for your event pages. You can add your logo, choose colors that match your brand, customize the layout, and add custom content. Higher-tier plans offer more advanced customization options, including custom CSS and white labeling.

What kind of support do you offer?

We offer different levels of support based on your plan. All customers have access to our comprehensive knowledge base and email support. Professional plan users receive priority support with faster response times. Enterprise customers enjoy 24/7 premium support and a dedicated account manager to assist with their specific needs.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. If you upgrade, the new features will be immediately available, and you'll be billed the prorated difference. If you downgrade, the changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
